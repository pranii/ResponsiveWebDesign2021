HOME

We decided to dedicate our new Project „FAMILY“ to all the children in the world. Our Main Goal is creating a safe place for kids to grow up in and develop an autonomous life.
To support our mission we came up with keychains that represent the behaviour of people that are subjected to a toxic enviroment in their childhood.
Our proceeds will go into research, because we believe that once this phenomenon is really understood, we have a better chance of creating a safe enviroment.
Together we can shape a better future for our children.
And as the philosophy of rreth implies, our children will therefore make our world a better place once they feel safe in it.








ABOUT US

Rreth is an organisation that consists of five members. We made it our mission to talk about subjects that usually get swept under the rug. As the name implies (rreth: (alb) circle) we want to break circles of abuse. The project FAMILY is inspired by Alexander McQueens Fashionshow “VOSS” in which he displayed the narcissism behind the fashion industry.

We tried to abstract his message and implement it in our work, to conceptualise the narcissistic abuse inside a dysfunctional family system, and to show how narcissism can be developed even as a child and how the abuse impacts a person in within their first interaction with the outer world.








PHILOSOPHY

RETH means CIRCLE and we believe that every circle can be broken once it is understood. Therefore we want people to develop personal boundaries so that they wont find themselves in cirlcles of abuse. …/

To give you an idea of how children are developed in unhealthy enviroments we will give you a small instruction on how survival strategies work.








fight

Narcissistic defense against an outside thread

driven by the belief that power and control can create safety assuage abandonment and secure love.
These types learn to respond to their feelings of abandonment with anger and subsequently use contempt, a toxic amalgam of narcissistic rage and disgust, to intimidate and shame others into mirroring them and into acting as extensions of themselves.
/there is no safety
/may become sociopathic, ranging along a continuum


flight

Obsessive Compulsive Defense

As children, flight types respond to their family trauma by relentlessly fleeing the inner pain of their abandonment and lack of attachment with the symbolic flight of constant busyness.
/ obsessively and compulsively driven by the unconscious belief that perfection will make them safe and loveable
/ safety lies in solitude


freeze

Dissociative Defense

Usually the most abandoned child „lost child“.
This defense develop around classical dissociation, which allows them to disconnect from experiencing their abandonment pain.
/ believe people and danger are synonymous
/ safety lies in solitude


fawn

Codependent Defense

narcissistic parent uses them to press them into service.
scaring and shaming them out of developing a healthy sense of self.
/ seek safety by merging with others
